How It Works

Your Journey in 5 Simple Steps

01

Onboarding

Enter your degree, career goals, and current skill level. The AI builds a fully personalized learning roadmap and skill milestones just for you.

02

Daily & Weekly Learning

Receive AI-generated daily exercises, weekly quizzes, and bi-weekly mini-projects. The AI adjusts if you struggle or excel.

03

Project Guidance

Every 2 weeks, get a real-world project with requirements, resources, and step-by-step hints. AI evaluates your submission automatically.

04

AI Mock Interviews

Practice with an AI voice agent that conducts real mock interviews and gives feedback on communication, technical answers, and confidence.

05

Career Launch

AI generates your resume, suggests job opportunities, and gives you an interview readiness score when you're ready to apply.

06

Earn & Level Up

Unlock badges, maintain daily streaks, climb the leaderboard, and earn certificates as you complete milestones and projects.
