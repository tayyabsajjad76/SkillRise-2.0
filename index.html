About the Platform

Skill Rise 2.0 — Your AI-Powered
Degree-to-Job Mentor

Unlike traditional online courses, Skill Rise 2.0 is an intelligent platform that acts as your personal mentor — guiding you from the very start of your academic journey to landing your first job.

It combines personalized roadmaps, daily learning plans, weekly assessments, AI mock interviews, resume building, and career guidance — all in one adaptive system that evolves with your progress.

How It Works

Your Journey in 5 Simple Steps

01

Onboarding

Enter your degree, career goals, and current skill level. The AI builds a fully personalized learning roadmap and skill milestones just for you.

02

Daily & Weekly Learning

Receive AI-generated daily exercises, weekly quizzes, and bi-weekly mini-projects. The AI adjusts if you struggle or excel.

03

Project Guidance

Career Launch

06

Earn & Level Up

Unlock badges, maintain daily streaks, climb the leaderboard, and earn certificates as you complete milestones and projects.

Platform Features

Everything You Need to Succeed

Nine powerful AI-driven modules that work together to take you from student to professional.

AI Mentor Dashboard

See your full personalized roadmap, daily learning streaks, skill mastery levels, and your predicted interview readiness score — all in one place.

Daily & Weekly Planner

AI generates daily exercises, readings, weekly quizzes, and bi-weekly mini-projects. Adapts when you struggle — advances when you excel.

AI Mock Interview

AI voice agent conducts real mock interviews. Get instant feedback on your communication, technical accuracy, and confidence level.

Project Workshop

Every 2 weeks, receive a real-world project with full requirements, curated resources, and hints. AI automatically evaluates your submission.

AI Resume Builder

AI analyzes your completed skills, projects, and assessments to generate a professional CV — tailored to the jobs you want to apply for.

Analytics Dashboard

Track quiz scores, project completions, skill mastery, and learning streaks. AI predicts when you'll be job-ready based on real data.

Resource Assistant

AI curates YouTube tutorials, blogs, documentation, and cheat sheets tailored exactly to your current learning level and topic.

AI Partner & Motivator

An intelligent chatbot/voice agent that acts as your mentor, motivator, and reminder system — with adaptive personality that encourages you when stuck.

Smart Course Suggestions

AI dynamically shows you "the next course you should take" based on your progress, goals, and skill gaps — never waste time on the wrong content.

Our Vision

An Influential Educational Institution

Working ethically with emerging technologies for positive social, economic, and community impact.

We will achieve this by being innovative, relevant, and responsive to the needs of industry — ensuring our graduate outcomes are led by quality academic excellence and economic development.

Our Mission

The Skill Rise Institute Will

1

Be recognised as contributing to the successful adoption and evolution of blockchain and related technologies ("Web3") among the Youth.

2

Cater to international and domestic students who are seeking to access a rapidly growing area of employment, innovation and entrepreneurial contributions to society.

3

Equip students to be lifelong learners, adapting to changes in the business environment and emerging technologies.

Core Values

Six Values That Drive Us

Care

Recognising and affirming the efforts and contributions of each person as they participate and develop in their skills.

Curiosity

Attentive and open-minded exploration of the current world and the possibilities of technology to make it better.

Creativity

Bold and innovative thinking that explores change without being constrained by what is currently possible.

Celebration

Actively seeking out positive contributions and energetically affirming them across our community.

Commitment

Resilient determination and passion to do hard things and make a positive difference in students' lives.

Courage

Bold engagement with difficult thinking and connecting to achieve high impact outcomes for students and society.
